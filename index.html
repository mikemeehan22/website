About Me

My name is Michael Meehan and I have been in education for 8 years. Currently I am teaching 8th grade social studies at West Lake Middle School in Apex, North Carolina. I am also working towards a masters degree in Learning Design & Technology from North Carolina State University. You can find more about the program here.
